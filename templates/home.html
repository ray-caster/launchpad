Our Story

From humble beginnings to reaching for the stars - our journey of growth and impact.

From Classroom Club to Community Platform

Launchpad.org is an independent, student-founded organization that grew out of the Cita Hati Robotics Club — evolving from a small school initiative into a mission-driven platform for teaching robotics and technology to a wider community.

Our goal is to empower the next generation of innovators by equipping them with hands-on skills in electronics, coding, and system design — and guiding them to use those skills to solve real problems that help others.

Our Vision

To create a world where technology education is accessible to all, and where young innovators are empowered to build solutions that make a difference.
